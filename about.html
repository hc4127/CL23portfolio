The Basics!

I was born and raised in Forest Hills, Queens, NYC to two Taiwanese parents (I'm from where Peter Parker is from!). After taking Kindergarten in New York, I moved to to a small little town called Beimen in Tainan, Taiwan. That place was very barren, but I think that incentivized me to be more creative in order to entertain myself. I made a lot of action figure stop motion movies and comic books as a kid, that's probably where I got my love for creativity from. After Beimen, I moved around Taiwan for a few years but eventually landed in Shanhua, Tainan, which was slightly less depressing and barren than Beimen.

High School!

I hated school! It was genuinely the worst thing for a kid with ADHD to sit in an Asian classroom. The only thing I like about my high school was number 1: going home; number 2: my science club. I created a small little science club of around 15 people back in junior year of high school. I wanted a club that deviated from all the other nerdy clubs, one that incentivized creativity and innovation! I consider those kiddos my family. I try not to forget where I came from even when I'm on the other side of the world from them. The picture on the right has nothing to do with my school by the way, it's just a pretty picture of Tainan because my school is so ghetto there's no pictures on Google of it. (Not that I would want to taint my website with pictures of it anyway)

NYU Abu Dhabi!

After all those years of terrible terrible high school, I find myself once again in another "crossroads of the world." I've come here not intending to be a STEM major like I always thought I would be. I'm here to do what I actually love: creative stuff. That's why I'm set on majoring in Interactive media. I could not be happier to be here. I've met some of the coolest people in my life in the past few months and I know I'm bound to meet even more on my journey through NYUAD.
