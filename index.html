About Me

I was born and raised in Forest Hills, Queens, NYC to two Taiwanese parents (I'm from where Peter Parker is from!). After taking Kindergarten in New York, I moved to to a small little town called Beimen in Tainan, Taiwan. That place was very barren, but I think that incentivized me to be more creative in order to entertain myself. Right now I'm a freshman at NYUAD, intending to major in Interactive Media and gathering the skills I need for my dream career: opening a game studio!

Newest Project!

Not Everyone Wants to Wear the Mask. Step into the world of 'Friendly Neighborhood', where the line between ordinary and extraordinary blurs in the life of Pavitr Prabhakar, a 19-year-old NYU Abu Dhabi student. It's not just about the big heroic gestures; it's about the small, everyday decisions that shape a true hero.
